# Partial Proxy-Study Summary

This archive captures the stopped `paper_proxy_2048` proxy-study run as of 2026-04-10 after completing `nca`, `lime`, and `simpler_tasks` and before finishing `procedural`, `dyck`, and `summarization`. The run used `EleutherAI/pythia-160m-deduped`, context length `2048`, seeds `11/23/37`, a total compute budget set to `2x` the original first full-run budget, and a synthetic pre-pretraining budget constrained to `2%` of total suite compute. Shared downstream and natural-warmup budgets were `1764` and `882` steps respectively; synthetic budgets were token-matched across mechanisms, which gave `72` synthetic steps for the `2048`-length `nca`/`dyck` runs and `288` synthetic steps for the `512`-length `lime`/`simpler_tasks`/`procedural`/`summarization` runs. Downstream datasets remained mechanism-specific: `general_text` used `HuggingFaceFW/fineweb-edu`, `math_text` used `HuggingFaceTB/finemath`, and `summary_text` used `vblagoje/cc_news`. Across the completed mechanisms, no positive transfer signal appeared: `nca` contradicted both transfer and compute-matched claims (`scratch 6.11`, `transferred 6.69`, `baseline 5.79` mean eval loss), `lime` contradicted both more strongly (`4.92`, `6.17`, `4.48`), and `simpler_tasks` was near scratch but still slightly worse and clearly worse than the compute-matched baseline (`6.11`, `6.14`, `5.78`). Convergence claims were not met within budget. The original free-generation reasoning/algorithmic probes were degenerate in this regime; a later arithmetic/needle candidate-scoring fix landed mid-run, so probe results for already-persisted early seeds should be read cautiously even though the main loss-based conclusions above are stable.
